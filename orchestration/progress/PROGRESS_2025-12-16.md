## 4. Benchmark Updates

| Model | Quant | Size | Baseline | Optimized | Method |
|-------|-------|------|----------|-----------|--------|
| Qwen3-Coder-53B-A3B | Q4_K_M | 30GB | 18.54 t/s | **27.9 t/s** | MoE 4 experts (+50%) |
| Qwen3-Coder-53B-A3B | Q4_K_M | 30GB | 18.54 t/s | N/A | Spec decode (8.96% accept - too low) |

**Key Finding:** Qwen3-Coder-53B cannot use Qwen2.5 drafts (token mismatch) and has low acceptance with Qwen3-0.6B (8.96%). MoE expert reduction is the only effective optimization.
